Books
Old Testament Theology by Paul House
Rebekah: Great introduction to Old Testament theology that surveys each book of the OT and explains how the book reveals a different facet of God and His plan.
Learn More
War and Peace by Tolstoy
Ryan: As the title implies, this masterpiece of literature covers each area of life and in the end reminds you of what is ultimately most important for our time here on earth.
Learn More
To the Golden Shore by Courtney Anderson
Rebekah: This missionary biography covers the life of Adoniram Judson and his ministry in Burma. This book not only pulls you into the lives of Judson and his family throughout all of their victories and trials, but also highlights God's hand in it all. You will not only learn more about the life of this great missionary but you will also be forced to wrestle through issues such as suffering, family, death, agnosticism, and more
Learn More
Other recommendations:
Whole Christ by Sinclair Ferguson
Ryan: A powerful read which shows how legalism and licentiousness are two sides of the same coin: our failure to remember that God's law is not separate from His goodness, but rather an essential part of it
Sermons and Testimonies
Rosaria Butterfield's Testimony
Rosaria Butterfield was a English Literature professor at Syracuse on the path to becoming tenured professor when God intervened in her life and saved her. A powerful testimony that speaks to the importance of prayer, community, and having faith in the power of the Holy Spirit in a person's life.
Learn More
The Big Picture of God's Mission by Dr. Abner Chou
Dr. Chou covers the whole Bible in an hour, demonstrating the cohesion of the Bible and how understanding biblical theology impacts our lives. I have heard of at least one student transferring from another Bible college to Master's after hearing this, due to the fact that they learned more in this one talk than they had in the their entire time at the other school.
Learn More
Depth of the Gospel by Paul Washer
Paul Washer addresses a college audience here, but the message of the gospel that he preaches is applicable to all. Both of us were convicted by how trivially we think of the gospel at times and we are sure you will be too. Part 2 is definitely worth your time as well.
