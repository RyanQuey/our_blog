How to Donate

A percentage of every donation goes to the missions board. However, all of the donation (including this percentage) is used for ministry and goes to a good place. Ryan talks about it more here.

All donations are tax deductible and are accepted with the understanding that Action International Ministries has complete control and administration of their use.

We’ve provided the most important information here. But for additional details on ACTION donation policies and other donation options, click here.

To Donate Online:

To donate online, click here then click the “Give” button.

You can give either a one-time donation or set up a recurring donation. Note that using a credit card or debit card means that about 3% + $0.50 goes to the credit card/debit card company. Additionally, if your card expires, it would have to be renewed. Automatic payment through a checking account (an “eCheck“) has only 0.5% + $0.50 fee. In other words, for $100 donation, a donation through credit card or debit card has $3.50 go to the bank, whereas a donation through eCheck has $1 go to the bank. Nevertheless, credit cards and debit cards are still a valid option. To do either method of payment, click on the link above.
