Hello! This is us:

Our wedding day celebrated with our church brothers and sisters! We got married in January 2017 and are planning to leave for Cambodia in the latter half of this year to strengthen the church there and eventually, once we learn the language well enough, to do pastoral training.
For now, we are working to prepare for Cambodia financially, working to build a strong foundation in our marriage, learning Khmer (the Cambodian language), and serving at our church, Valley Bible Church in Northridge, CA.

We are being sent through Action International, click here. Action International Ministries (ACTION) is an evangelical, interdenominational missionary-sending agency that works in Africa, Asia, Europe, and Latin America. Their strategy for urban ministry involves evangelism, discipleship, and development. ACTION networks with local churches, national organizations and other mission agencies to reach people for Christ, and seeks to train them in Christian living as well as assist them in their physical and economic needs.
