Pray for Us

For our most recent prayer requests, check our latest update here. You can also sign up to receive our email updates, which include any recent news and will alert you to any new updates we post here on this blog. But in general, please pray:

  • That we would grow more and more to become people who are humble and contrite and tremble at His word.
  • That our marriage would grow stronger and its strength not grounded in our circumstances but in our commitment to God and each other.
  • That we would have boldness in our proclamation of the Word.
  • For our involvement within the Khmer local church to build up the body there.
  • For Ryan's teaching at the Bible School to be clear and helpful for the students as they go on to serve in their local congregations.

Support the Translation and Publishication of Khmer Christian Resources
Our teammates in the ACTION translation team provide much-needed resources for a church that is desperate for solid, biblical truth, but is surrounded by false teaching on all sides. Gifts towards this project help offset costs involved in translation, printing, and publication process, as well as for their blog which is an aid to pastors and believers across the country.
